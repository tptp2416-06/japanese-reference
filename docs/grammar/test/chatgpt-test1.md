Quick Summary {#quick-summary}

<div class="ref-summary">
<h3>Mental Model</h3>
<ul>
<li>You don’t need to do everything—just do this, and that’s enough.</li>
</ul>
<h3>When This Actually Gets Used</h3>
<ul>
<li>Calming someone down</li>
<li>Reducing pressure</li>
<li>Giving gentle, non-pushy advice</li>
<li>Encouraging progress without perfection</li>
</ul>
</div>

Core Meaning {#core-meaning}

<div class="sb-wrap">

Core Rule: “If you do X, that’s enough—no need to overdo or worry.”

<div class="translation-pair">
<span class="nat-eng">Natural English:</span> You can just take it at your own pace—that’s enough.<br>
<div class="literal-line">Literal: If you go doing it at your own pace, it is good.</div>
</div>

<ruby>自分<rt>じぶん</rt></ruby>のペースでやればいい。

How It Works {#how-it-works}

<div class="sb-wrap">
<p>This pattern frames an action as “sufficient,” removing pressure and signaling that nothing more is required.</p>

Common Pairings

<ul>
<li><ruby>自分<rt>じぶん</rt></ruby>のペースで〜</li>
<li><ruby>少<rt>すこ</rt></ruby>しずつ〜</li>
<li><ruby>無理<rt>むり</rt></ruby>しないで〜</li>
</ul>

Tone and Distance Control

<ul>
<li>Softer than direct advice</li>
<li>Implies “this is enough,” not “you should do this”</li>
<li>Adding よ → friendly tone</li>
<li>Adding から → emotional reassurance</li>
</ul>

Micro Contrasts

<ul>

Why Not Use X Instead?

<div class="note-callout">
Using 〜たほうがいい here would feel more directive and less emotionally supportive.
</div>

<ul>

Common Mistakes

<div class="sb-wrap">

Don’t confuse with:

<div class="minimal-pair">

行いけばいい → suggestion
行いってもいい → permission

</div>
<div class="translation-pair">
Natural: “You can just go” vs “You’re allowed to go”<br>
<div class="literal-line">Literal: If you go, it’s good vs Even if you go, it’s good</div>
</div>
</div>

⸻

Register and Formality

<div class="sb-wrap">
<div class="register-block casual">
<ruby>やればいいよ</ruby><br>
Natural: Just do it—it’s enough<br>
<span class="shift-label">Shift:</span> Friendly and soft
</div>
<div class="register-block polite">
<ruby>やればいいです</ruby><br>
Natural: You can just do that<br>
<span class="shift-label">Shift:</span> Neutral polite
</div>
<div class="register-block awareness">
<ruby>すればよろしいです</ruby><br>
Natural: That would be sufficient<br>
<span class="shift-label">Shift:</span> Formal / [N2 — awareness only]
</div>
</div>

Hidden Points

<div class="sb-wrap">
<ul>
<li>“いい” expresses sufficiency, not approval</li>
<li>ば-condition gives logical framing</li>
<li>から adds emotional reassurance layer</li>
<li>Often implies “don’t overthink it”</li>
</ul>
</div>
